    How this works

    Stuck staring at your rack? Drop in a screenshot of your Crossplay game and we'll read the board and tray, then hand you the best plays — ranked by points and by what's smart to keep.

    1. Load a board — upload a screenshot (or tap Load example to kick the tires).
    2. Check the read — we guess the tiles; tap any cell that's off to fix it.
    3. Find best plays — tap a play to watch it light up on the board.

    It all runs right here in your browser — nothing leaves your phone. Not affiliated with the NYT; just a friendly helper.
